Common questions

How are entries sourced?

Every entry summarises publicly available material from US courts (Supreme Court of the United States, US Courts), federal agencies (USPTO, Copyright Office, FTC, SEC, DOL, EEOC, FCC, IRS, DOJ), legal-reference works (Cornell Legal Information Institute, ABA Public Education, Restatement of Contracts, UCC, Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, Federal Rules of Evidence), and Library of Congress materials. The full list is in the in-app About screen.

What jurisdiction does JB Law cover?

JB Law is focused on US federal and state law. State-specific differences are flagged where relevant, but entries are written from a US perspective. A UK companion may follow in a separate app.

I noticed a term that should be added or corrected.

Please send the term and what you'd like to see covered to the email above. Suggestions and corrections are welcomed and reviewed for future updates.

Why don't my favourites sync to my other device?

JB Law stores favourites locally on each device and does not have a server or account system, so favourites do not sync between devices. iCloud app-data backup at the system level may carry them across as part of a device restore.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstall the app. JB Law collects nothing and stores only your starred favourites locally; uninstalling removes them.

Is this legal advice?

No. JB Law is reference material for general orientation. It is not legal advice. Laws vary by jurisdiction and change over time. Always consult a licensed attorney for guidance on a specific legal matter.
