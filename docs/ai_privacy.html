JB AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

JB AI is a reference glossary for the language of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and the silicon underneath. It is built to work without collecting any information about you.

What we collect

Nothing. JB AI collects no personal information, no usage analytics, no device identifiers, and no data of any kind from anyone using the app.

What stays on your device

The app stores one thing locally, on your device only, using iOS UserDefaults:

  • The list of glossary terms you have starred as favourites.

This information never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us or any third party. It is removed when you delete the app.

Third-party services

JB AI uses no third-party SDKs, no analytics services, no advertising frameworks, and no crash reporters. The app does not contact any server we operate, because we do not operate one.

Children

JB AI is rated 4+ in the App Store and is suitable for all ages. As above, no data is collected from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes materially, the updated text will appear on this page with a new revision date.
