Common questions

How are entries sourced?

Every entry summarises publicly available information from authoritative sources — FDA, NIH, NCI Dictionary of Cancer Terms, NHGRI, WHO, EMA, and others. The full list is in the in-app About screen (open the filter circle, tap "About JB Pharma" at the bottom).

I noticed a term that should be added or corrected.

Please send the term and what you'd like to see covered to the email above. Suggestions and corrections are welcomed and reviewed for future updates.

Why don't all my favorites sync to my other device?

JB Pharma stores favorites locally on each device and does not have a server or account system, so favorites do not sync between devices. iCloud app-data backup at the system level may carry them across as part of a device restore.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstall the app. JB Pharma collects nothing and stores only your starred favorites locally; uninstalling removes them.

Is this medical advice?

No. JB Pharma is reference material for the language of pharma and healthcare. It is for orientation, not for clinical decision-making. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical questions.
