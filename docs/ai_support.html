Common questions

How are entries sourced?

Every entry summarises publicly available information from frontier AI labs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Meta AI / FAIR, Stanford CRFM, Allen Institute for AI), hardware vendors (NVIDIA, AMD, Intel, TSMC, ASML, Apple Silicon), standards bodies (IEEE, ACM, MLCommons, Open Compute Project), and original research papers. The full list is in the in-app About screen.

I noticed a term that should be added or corrected.

Please send the term and what you'd like to see covered to the email above. Suggestions and corrections are welcomed and reviewed for future updates.

Why don't my favourites sync to my other device?

JB AI stores favourites locally on each device and does not have a server or account system, so favourites do not sync between devices. iCloud app-data backup at the system level may carry them across as part of a device restore.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstall the app. JB AI collects nothing and stores only your starred favourites locally; uninstalling removes them.

Is this engineering or investment advice?

No. JB AI is reference material for the language of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and the silicon underneath. It is for orientation, not for engineering, investment, or any other professional decision-making. Always consult qualified professionals for technical or financial questions.
